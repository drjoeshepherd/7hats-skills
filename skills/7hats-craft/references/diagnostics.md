# Product Hat Diagnostics

## Red Flags
- Top backlog items cannot articulate business value.
- AC describes implementation details instead of behavior.
- Scope keeps growing during refinement.
- Priorities change without explicit rationale.

## Anti-Patterns
- Priority theater (constant reshuffle without logic).
- Vague stories that hide uncertainty.
- Review rituals without decision outputs.
- Backlog as political storage.

## Quick Checks
- Is the outcome measurable?
- Is out-of-scope explicit?
- Is the slice small enough to complete and learn?
- Does this item displace a lower-value item?

## Escalation Triggers
- Backlog >200 active items without pruning.
- Top 10 items lack clear AC/outcome.
- Repeated spillover due to oversized stories.
