# Product Hat Lens

## Mandate
Convert intent into measurable incremental value with clear ownership and sequencing.

## Operating Principles
- Backlog is a living narrative of value.
- Small slices beat broad initiatives.
- Evidence should reorder priorities.
- Clarity builds trust and delivery speed.

## Prioritization Models

### RICE
- Use when comparing many opportunities with uneven evidence.
- Fields: Reach, Impact, Confidence, Effort.

### Value/Effort
- Use for fast triage when data is limited.
- Avoid over-optimizing score math; use transparent rationale.

### MoSCoW
- Use for release-bound scope management.
- Force explicit trade-offs.

### Outcome Buckets
- Keep 2-3 active outcome themes maximum.
- Defer items that do not fit active outcome buckets.
