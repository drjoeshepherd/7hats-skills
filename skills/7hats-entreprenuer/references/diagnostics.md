# Entrepreneur Hat Diagnostics

## Red Flags
- Large initiatives without minimum proof.
- No explicit kill criteria.
- Portfolio overloaded with high-effort low-urgency bets.
- Resource allocation follows politics instead of evidence.

## Anti-Patterns
- Scale before fit.
- Metric soup with no decision utility.
- Over-diversification of active bets.
- "Hobby horse" initiatives with no review gates.

## Quick Checks
- What evidence would stop this investment?
- What is the next smallest proof step?
- Are we allocating more than evidence justifies?
