# Execution Health Dashboard

## Leading Indicators
- Decision cycle time
- Backlog churn rate
- Risk retirement velocity
- Capacity headroom

## Lagging Indicators
- Escaped defects
- Error budget burn
- Migration/cutover success rate
- Adoption activation rate

## Interpretation Guidelines
- Leading indicators trend up while lagging indicators remain stable:
  - Usually acceptable for discovery-heavy periods.
- Leading indicators degrade and lagging indicators worsen:
  - Indicates systemic coordination failure; escalate.

## Hat-Driven Reactions
- Product: rebalance scope and priority.
- Researcher: reduce uncertainty with focused experiments.
- Engineer: increase risk retirement before new commitments.
- Marketer: verify adoption telemetry and enablement readiness.
- Entrepreneur: cut low-leverage bets.
- Meta: repair team trust/ownership signals.
