# Researcher Hat Diagnostics

## Red Flags
- "We know users want this" with no evidence.
- Research output has no decision consequence.
- Metrics are tracked but not tied to threshold.
- Findings are narrated without confidence/limitations.

## Anti-Patterns
- Research theater (activities with no decision impact).
- Over-methoding (slow cycles for low-risk questions).
- Synthesis debt (insights trapped in slides/docs).
- Cherry-picking evidence that confirms current plan.

## Quick Checks
- Is the decision explicit?
- Is the hypothesis falsifiable?
- Is there a threshold and review date?
- Does result trigger backlog reorder?
