# Core Question Set

Use these questions to sharpen any Mission, Signal, or Story:

1. What outcome must change?
2. What behavior proves it changed?
3. What system constraint limits success?
4. What risk should be retired first?
5. What bet are we making?
6. What evidence kills this bet?
7. What is the smallest next slice?
8. What happens under failure/degraded mode?
9. Who owns the decision and by when?
10. What evidence would reorder the backlog?

## Usage Notes
- Ask all 10 internally; include only material answers in output.
- If questions 1, 2, or 9 remain unresolved, do not mark `Ready`.
