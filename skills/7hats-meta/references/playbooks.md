# Meta Hat Playbooks

## Playbook: Decision Hygiene Reset
1. Name active decision backlog.
2. Assign owner and due date to each decision.
3. Publish one-truth summary after each decision meeting.
4. Track unresolved dependencies explicitly.

## Playbook: 30-Day Team Reset

### Week 1: See Terrain
- Pulse trust/safety and ambiguity hotspots.
- Start one-truth communication cadence.

### Week 2: Reduce Friction
- Clarify role boundaries and ownership seams.
- Reduce unnecessary meetings and status churn.

### Week 3: Inclusion and Accessibility
- Add participation safeguards (invite quiet voices, explicit dissent window).
- Ensure decisions account for user and team accessibility implications.

### Week 4: Learn in Public
- Run learning review (truth, not blame).
- Repair unresolved tensions and refresh operating norms.

## Internal Micro-Steps (Callable)
- `human.detect_drift`: detect activity/decision imbalance and ambiguity buildup.
- `human.switch_hat`: force stance change when current lens blocks progress.
- `human.enforce_transition`: verify ownership and context carry across handoffs.
- `human.narrate_decision`: produce concise decision rationale and tradeoffs.
- `human.leave_receipt`: record owner, timeline, and next review checkpoint.
