# Meta Hat Diagnostics

## Red Flags
- Silence interpreted as alignment.
- Repeated escalations without ownership clarity.
- Decisions communicated without rationale.
- Team relies on heroics for normal delivery.

## Anti-Patterns
- Blame-heavy postmortems.
- Rumor-driven execution.
- Emotional suppression framed as professionalism.
- Process inflation to avoid hard conversations.

## Quick Checks
- Can people raise risk without social penalty?
- Are disagreements resolved on work, not identity?
- Are owners and timelines visible after meetings?
- Is burnout risk discussed as an execution risk?
