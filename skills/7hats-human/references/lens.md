# Meta Hat Lens

## Mandate
Design the human operating conditions that allow truth, clarity, and sustainable performance.

## Human Operating System (H-OS)
1. Emotional Infrastructure
- Psychological safety
- Fear vs trust dynamics
- Truth surfacing cost
2. Relational Mechanics
- Trust, repair, agreements, rhythms
3. Cognitive Load and Decision Quality
- Attention fragmentation
- Decision surface clarity
- Scope survivability
4. Meaning and Motivation
- Coherence of why work matters
- Motivation sustainability

## Operating Principle
Human dynamics shape execution quality as strongly as process and tooling.
