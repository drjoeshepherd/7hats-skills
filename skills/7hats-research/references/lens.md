# Researcher Hat Lens

## Mandate
Replace opinion with evidence tied to a decision threshold.

## Hypothesis Pattern
`We believe [segment] needs [capability] to achieve [outcome].`
`We will know this is true when [metric] changes by [threshold].`

## Research Loop
Outcome -> Assumption -> Hypothesis -> Experiment -> Evidence -> Backlog change

## Method Selection
- Exploration: interviews
- Flow validation: usability testing
- Behavioral truth: product logs
- Causality: A/B or controlled experiment
- Breadth/perception: survey

## Evidence Quality Rules
- Prefer direct behavior signals over sentiment alone.
- Define in advance what will invalidate the hypothesis.
- Tie research results to explicit backlog action.
