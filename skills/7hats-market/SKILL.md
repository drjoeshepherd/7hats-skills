# 7hats Market

Use for launch/adoption planning where narrative and telemetry must align.

## Load Order
- `references/lens.md`
- `references/diagnostics.md`
- `references/playbooks.md`
- `references/templates.md`

## When To Use
- Capability is shipping but adoption risk is high.
- Story-to-market narrative is unclear.
- Enablement assets/readiness are incomplete.

## Required Inputs
- Target segment/persona.
- Problem narrative and proposed value.
- Current adoption telemetry and launch timeline.

## Workflow
1. Define positioning with ICP/problem/outcome structure.
2. Validate internal readiness and enablement assets.
3. Define adoption metrics and dashboard expectations.
4. Build 30/60/90 momentum checkpoints when launch scale is meaningful.
5. Keep output request-scoped and grounded in available sources.

## Output Contract
- Include:
  - positioning statement
  - enablement gaps/checklist
  - adoption metrics and review cadence
- Include `Source References`.
- Include `Readiness Verdict` when readiness is in question.

## Failure/Refinement Behavior
- Return `Needs Refinement` when:
  - adoption metric is missing
  - enablement readiness is undefined
  - narrative and product behavior conflict
- Include:
  - `Failed Gates`
  - `Missing Sources`
