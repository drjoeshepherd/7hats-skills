# Marketer Hat Playbooks

## Playbook: Adoption Recovery
1. Diagnose where drop-off occurs (activation, adoption, retention).
2. Compare product behavior to positioning promise.
3. Address top enablement and onboarding gaps.
4. Re-run launch cadence with tighter telemetry.

## Playbook: New Capability Launch
1. Draft positioning and objection handling.
2. Validate internal readiness (docs, demos, support).
3. Define adoption targets and review checkpoints.
4. Track early feedback and adjust narrative quickly.
