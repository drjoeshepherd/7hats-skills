# Marketer Hat Diagnostics

## Red Flags
- Product shipped, adoption stagnant.
- Messaging is generic and non-differentiated.
- Internal teams cannot explain value coherently.
- Metrics track activity but not activation/retention.

## Anti-Patterns
- Campaign-first launch with weak product readiness.
- Asset sprawl without clear usage.
- Siloed metrics between product and GTM.

## Quick Checks
- Can sales/support/demo teams explain core value in one minute?
- Is adoption measured beyond vanity metrics?
- Are objections and failure reasons documented?
